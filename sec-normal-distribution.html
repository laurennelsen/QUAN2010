    Exercise 5.1.5.

    Let \(z\) be the standard normal variable. Find the following:

    (a)

    \(P(Z\geq -2.89)\text{.}\)
    Answer.
    \(P(Z\geq -2.89)=0.9980737908678121\text{.}\)

    (b)

    \(P(0.11\lt Z\lt 2.7)\text{.}\)
    Answer.
    \(P(0.11\lt Z\lt 2.7)=0.45273771365464255\text{.}\)

    (c)

    \(z^*\) such that \(P(Z\leq z^*)=0.61\text{.}\)
    Answer.
    \(z^*=0.2793190344474541\text{.}\)

    (d)

    \(z^*\) such that \(P(-z^*\lt Z\lt z^*)=0.69\text{.}\)
    Answer.
    \(z^*=1.015222033217028\text{.}\)
